Mattia Sensi

Sophia Antipolis – Méditerranée, Byron building. Email: mattia.sensi@inria.fr

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Inria Sophia Antipolis. I joined the group MathNeuro, led by Prof. Mathieu Desroches, in December 2021.
My main research interests are dynamical systems, mathematical biology and geometric singular perturbation theory (GSPT). During my PhD, I primarily worked on various epidemics models, and my current main project is on mathematical models of neural activity, specifically multiple time scales modeling of differential neurotransmitter release.
