Mattia Sensi

Sophia Antipolis – Méditerranée, Byron building. mattiasensei@gmail.com

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Inria Sophia Antipolis. I joined the group MathNeuro, led by Prof. Mathieu Desroches, in December 2021.
My main research interests are dynamical systems, mathematical biology and geometric singular perturbation theory. During my PhD, I have mostly worked on various epidemics models, and my current project is mathematical models of neural activity, specifically multi-timescale modeling of differential neurotransmitter release.
